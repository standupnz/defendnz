Purchase Request Order Form:

Interested in picking up the complete outfit, or multiple packages? These items are provided for sale with little to no markup, this website seeks to make no profit from the provision of necessary equipment. However, to give simple quotes the package prices are rounded up to the nearest ten dollar figure, as a result some small savings can be made in larger purchases. The figures listed here as prices should be considered guidelines, and a final quote will be issued before any purchase is considered final. If you are particularly interested in a single item, or wish to arrange your own package from the complete set you can make a custom purchase request, however it is unlikely that the stock will be on hand to finalise your purchase. In this event a quote will be provided, along with an estimation as to the time it will take to make those items available.


It is important to note that where packages include sized items, your purchase cannot be finalised until the required measurements are received.

Thank you for considering purchasing these packages.
