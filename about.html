It is readily apparent to even the casual observer that the state of governance in New Zealand is intolerable to the general population. Unfortunately, the method of governance is designed against repair, and only serves to propagate the interests of those in power, and to use its power to corrupt those moral actors who enter governance in attempts to engage in repair. It is so deliberately and completely resistant to change due to absence of restrictions to government power. With no codified constitution, the common law of New Zealand subjugates the citizenry under absolute dominion of the state, and these powers are so deeply entrenched it is close to impossible to even dream of a peaceful and legislative repair to rule of law. Given this situation, the possibility of revolution should be considered to be certain, and to that end it is necessary that the people of New Zealand be prepared and equipped to effect, survive and succeed in the revolutionary efforts.

Therefore, the end goal and mission statement of this website is to institute a codified constitutional document that establishes the proper role of government, restricting the power of the state over and securing the freedom of the population as well as local council administrations. To this end, you will find items and information necessary to the effecting of revolution, either for sale or freely available through this website. On the 'Shop' page you will find equipment packages for sale, on the 'Train' page you will be able to reserve program places training attendants on how to use the equipment here available for purchase, how to survive outside of modern society and how to defend yourself with the use of martial arts, and on the 'Support' page you can make donations or volunteer your services if you are unable to contribute financially.

For more information please visit the 'Ask' page and review the FAQ, and if that still leaves you with questions you can submit them there and I will get back to you as soon as possible, adding your question to the FAQ if appropriate and with your consent. Thank you for your consideration.
