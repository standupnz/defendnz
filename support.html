While we are more than willing to accept donations, so much more than money we value volunteers who are willing to contribute not just their dollars, but their time and effort to propagate these ideas of liberty and justice for to all New Zealand citizens, to all New Zealand citizens. Any donations that are made will go entirely to equipment, either to discount the prices of packages on the 'Shop' page, outfit volunteers with necessary equipment or to facilitate and subsidise training events. If you are interested in volunteering, wish to make a donation, or simply contribute nothing more than a message please do so using the submission form here. Many thanks indeed to those that support us already and thank you to those here reading this considering doing so.
